Fire. Wood. Time.

An upscale wood-fired bistro where the ingredients are taken as seriously as the atmosphere.

Old-growth forest meets open kitchen.

Born from a love of the Pacific Northwest and the primal instinct of cooking over open flame. At Ember & Oak, we let the fire do the talking. Our wood-fired hearth is the heartbeat of the restaurant, infusing every dish with the subtle, unmistakable flavour of applewood and oak.

We partner with local foragers, coastal fishermen, and organic farmers to bring you a menu that changes with the wind, but remains steadfast in its dedication to quality.
